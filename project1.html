Not Paid Enough

Not Paid Enough takes place in the 1970s, inside a version of Area 51 where aliens are forced to work as cleaners without getting paid. Meanwhile, conspiracy theorists are more uncontrollable than ever, determined to prove the existence of little green men.

This was a group project involving 15 people, in which I worked as a 3D modeler, contributing to the creation of assets and the visual world of the game.

The game features first-person gameplay, where conspiracy theorists must take photos and collect evidence to score points within a limited time, while the alien janitor tries to stop them from succeeding.
