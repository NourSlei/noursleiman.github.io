Sci fi Environment

This solo project explores the creation of a cinematic sci-fi environment set inside a secure research facility. The focus was on building a believable atmosphere through lighting, composition, and detailed asset design, with the goal of immersing the viewer in a tense, futuristic setting.

The scene presents a modular laboratory space filled with custom-built elements, from experimental equipment to containment structures. Lighting plays a central role, combining neon accents with volumetric fog to shape the mood and guide the viewer’s attention. Subtle visual cues and set details suggest an underlying narrative, hinting at ongoing experiments and potential instability within the facility.
