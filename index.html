3D Artist

I’m a 3D artist with a professional bachelor’s degree in video games. I’m currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Management of Interactive 3D Technologies, where I focus on the intersection of art, technology, and immersive experiences.

I like shaping environments and assets that tell a story through detail and mood. I’m also exploring real-time 3D and virtual reality as ways to build more immersive and interactive worlds.

Outside of 3D, I keep it simple: sunsets, iced coffee, and llamas.
