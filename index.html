3D Artist

My journey started in fine arts, where I spent two years building a strong foundation in traditional techniques before transitioning into 3D. I’m currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Management of Interactive 3D Technologies, where I explore the intersection of art, technology, and immersive experiences. I like shaping environments and assets that tell a story through detail and mood. I’m also exploring real-time 3D and virtual reality as ways to build more immersive and interactive worlds.
